Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mob Shot ("the App") handles information when you use the game.

Information We Collect

Mob Shot is designed as a lightweight arcade game and does not require account creation, user registration, or direct submission of personal information in order to play.

The App itself is not intended to collect personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, or payment information.

Gameplay and Local Data

The App may store limited gameplay data locally on your device in order to support normal functionality, such as:

  • level progress
  • scores or star ratings
  • basic settings such as sound preferences

This information stays on your device unless your operating system backs it up through Apple services you use independently.

Analytics and Third-Party Services

At the time of this policy, Mob Shot is not intended to use third-party advertising SDKs, account systems, or external analytics tools inside the gameplay experience.

If future versions add analytics, advertising, cloud saves, or other connected services, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are broadly released.

Children's Privacy

Mob Shot is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law to provide personal data consent on their own. The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

Because the App is not designed to collect personal information directly, it does not intentionally sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep the App simple and minimize data handling. However, no software or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You can stop all collection of locally stored gameplay information by uninstalling the App from your device. Depending on your device settings, local data may also be removed when the App is deleted.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect gameplay changes, platform requirements, or legal obligations. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the support method provided on the App Store product page or official website.
