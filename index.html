Mob Shot

A stylish rooftop fruit shooter built around one-shot precision, moving city backdrops, and a playful noir underworld vibe. Aim fast, clear the fruit, and avoid the wrong target.

Genre Arcade Shooter
Mode Offline Solo Play

What Makes It Fun

Mob Shot mixes quick-session arcade controls with cinematic framing, exaggerated gunplay, and fruit targets balanced above stylized characters. Each level is designed to feel readable, punchy, and replayable.

Quick Skill Shots

Drag to aim, read the trajectory, and fire before the moment slips away. Every shot matters.

American City Backdrops

Progress through levels inspired by major U.S. cities, each with its own skyline rhythm, color palette, and silhouette landmarks.

Readable Arcade Challenge

Simple controls, clean objective design, and rising pressure make it easy to start and satisfying to improve.
